# APTITUDE SECTION (20 Questions)

## Question

Question: The sum of three consecutive odd numbers is 57. Find the smallest number.

A. 17
B. 18
C. 19
D. 21

Answer: A
Difficulty: Easy
Explanation: Let numbers be x, x+2, x+4. Sum = 3x+6=57 → 3x=51 → x=17.

A. 16.67 m/s
B. 15.5 m/s
C. 18.2 m/s
D. 20 m/s

Answer: A
Difficulty: Easy
Explanation: Speed = 240/4 = 60 km/h = 60 × (5/18) = 16.67 m/s.

A. 8:15
B. 6:15
C. 8:12
D. 6:12

Difficulty: Easy
